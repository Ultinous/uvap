UVAP Training

Ultinous offers hands-on training in UVAP.

The training typically takes two days and it is held at the Ultinous offices in Budapest, Hungary. Remote, online participation can be arranged.

The training covers overviews and exercises of UVAP and its components as well as various workshops and presentations.

To be able to participate in the training, knowledge of video analytics, Linux, and C# or Python is required.

For further information, requirements, dates, agenda, and registration see the Ultinous events page.
